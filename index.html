How to Compress Images Online

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "Select Image"
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = better quality)
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep original)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download your optimized image with reduced file size

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading - Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Better SEO rankings - Page speed is a ranking factor for search engines
  • Reduced bandwidth usage - Save on hosting costs and data transfer
  • Improved email deliverability - Smaller attachments are more likely to be delivered
  • Storage optimization - Store more images in less space
